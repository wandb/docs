The Weights & Biases mobile app keeps you connected to your machine learning experiments wherever you go. Track training runs in real time, explore your projects' histories, and follow your team's progress—all from your iPhone or iPad.

## Features

- **Track training runs**: View run status, metrics, and progress in real time
- **Explore project history**: Browse and search across your W&B projects
- **Stay informed**: Get updates on your experiments without opening your laptop
