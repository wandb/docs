Within your individual user account you can:
- Edit your profile, including your profile picture, display name, geographic location, biography information, and email addresses associated with your account.
- Configure API keys.
- Configure alerts for runs.
- Link your GitHub repository.
- Delete your account.

For more information, see [User settings](/platform/app/settings-page/user-settings/).

Use the team settings page to:
- Invite or remove new members from a team.
- Manage alerts for team runs.
- Change privacy settings.
- View and manage team storage usage.

For more information about team settings, see [Team settings](/platform/app/settings-page/teams/).
