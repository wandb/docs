<Info>
**Smart feature** control options are available for Enterprise and Pro accounts.

W&B honors exsiting MSA agreements. If your organization has an MSA agreement that includes specific data usage terms, W&B will adhere to those terms in relation to the use of your organization's data for AI features.

Contact your account executive or W&B support if you have questions about your MSA or data usage.
</Info>

Manage access to LLM-powered features and data sharing for product improvement within your organization. [W&B Admins](/platform/hosting/iam/access-management-intro#manage-users-and-teams-within-an-organization) can control how your organization's data is used by W&B's AI features, including the W&B Agent, with **Smart feature** settings.

To toggle **Smart feature** settings:

1. Navigate to the W&B App.
1. In the upper right corner of the page, select the **User menu** dropdown. Within the **Account** section of the dropdown, select **Settings**.
1. Within the **Settings** tab, select **Privacy**.
1. Scroll to the **Smart features** section. 
1. Toggle the options according to your preferences. Changes are saved automatically.
