title: "Governance and security"
description: "The following section describes how W&B Admins can control how your organization's data is used by W&B's AI features, including the W&B Agent."
---

<Info>
The following applies to Enterprise license and Pro plans.

Contact your account executive or W&B support if you have questions about your MSA or data usage.
</Info>

[W&B Admins](/platform/hosting/iam/access-management-intro#manage-users-and-teams-within-an-organization) can toggle settings to allow or disallow the following:

* **Enable AI-powered features**: Allow Customer Data to be processed by third parties to power AI features.
* **Use Customer Data for product improvement**: Allow W&B to use Customer Data to improve and build products. When off, data isn’t used by default, but users can still manually submit specific interactions through the UI.
* **Allow model training on Customer Data**: Allow W&B to use Customer Data to train AI models and improve performance over time.

<Note>
W&B defaults these toggle to OFF for Enterprise users with a pre-existing custom MSA that does not allow for sending their data to AI models, using their data for product improvement, or training AI on their data.

An Org Admin cannot adjust these settings unless they reach out to support to change their contract first.
</Note>

To toggle these settings:

1. Navigate to your organization's home page.
1. In the upper right corner of the page, select the **User menu** dropdown. Within the **Account** section of the dropdown, select **Settings**.
1. Within the **Settings** tab, select **Privacy**.
1. Scroll to the **Smart features** section. 
1. Toggle the options according to your preferences. Changes are saved automatically.
