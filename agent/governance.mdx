For existing customers, W&B conducts legal due diligence procedure with outside counsel to annotate whether your current MSA permits:

* Sending your data to Anthropic / Open AI
* Using your data for product improvement 
* Training models on top of your data

Contact your account executive or W&B support if you have questions about your MSA or data usage.


## Control data usage

[W&B Admins](/platform/hosting/iam/access-management-intro#manage-users-and-teams-within-an-organization) can control how their organization's data is used by W&B's AI features. Admins can choose to allow or disallow the following:

* **Enable LLM-powered features**: Controls whether your organization's W&B data may be processed by OpenAI and/or Anthropic to power AI features.
* **Share AI traces with W&B for improvement**: Permit W&B to use your shared data to improve AI models.
* **Allow model training on shared data**: Permit W&B to use your shared data to train AI models. Enabling this may improve future model quality. Learn more

To control your organization's data usage:

1. Navigate to your organization's home page.
1. In the upper right corner of the page, select the **User menu** dropdown. Within the **Account** section of the dropdown, select **Settings**.
1. Within the **Settings** tab, select **Privacy**.
1. Scroll to the **Smart features** section. 
1. Toggle the options according to your preferences. Changes are saved automatically.
