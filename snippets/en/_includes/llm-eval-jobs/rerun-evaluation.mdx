## Re-run an evaluation
To re-run the last evaluation again, follow the steps in [Evaluate your model](#evaluate-your-model). Select the destination project, then the model artifact details and the selected benchmarks you selected last time are populated automatically. Optionally, make adjustments, then start the evaluation.

To re-run a specific evaluation, you can import its configuration.

1. Follow the steps in [Evaluate your model](#evaluate-your-model). Stop after you select the destination project.
1. Click **Import configuration**.
1. Select the project that contains the previous evaluation results, then select the run.
1. Optionally adjust the coonfiguration.
1. Click **Launch**.
