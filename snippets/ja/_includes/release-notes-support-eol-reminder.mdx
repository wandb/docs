A W&B Server release is supported for 12 months from its initial release date. As a reminder, customers using [Self-managed](/ja/platform/hosting/hosting-options/self-managed/) are responsible to upgrade to a [supported release](/ref/release-notes/) in time to maintain support.

Refer to [Release policies and processes](/release-notes/release-policies). For assistance or questions, contact [support](mailto:support@wandb.com).
