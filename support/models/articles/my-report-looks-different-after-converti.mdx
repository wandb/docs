---
title: "My report looks different after converting from Markdown"
keywords: ["Reports", "Wysiwyg"]
---

The Markdown-to-WYSIWYG (what you see is what you get) conversion aims to maintain the original appearance of your report, but the process isn't flawless. If you notice discrepancies, report them so the team can evaluate the conversion. You can revert to the previous state until the editing session ends.
