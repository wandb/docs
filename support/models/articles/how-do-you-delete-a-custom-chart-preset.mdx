---
title: "How do you delete a custom chart preset?"
keywords: ["Charts"]
---

To delete a custom chart preset:

1. Access the custom chart editor.
2. Click the currently selected chart type to open a menu that displays all presets.
3. Hover over the preset to delete, then click the **Trash** icon.
