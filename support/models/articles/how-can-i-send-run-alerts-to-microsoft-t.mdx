---
title: "How can I send run alerts to Microsoft Teams?"
keywords: ["Alerts"]
---

W&B doesn't send alerts to Microsoft Teams directly, but you can route them through email so your team sees run notifications in a shared channel. To receive W&B alerts in Teams, follow these steps:

1. **Set up an email address for your Teams channel.** Create an email address for the Teams channel where you want to receive alerts.
2. **Forward W&B alert emails to the Teams channel's email address.** Configure W&B to send alerts by email, then forward these emails to your Teams channel's email.

After you complete these steps, W&B run alerts arrive in your Teams channel as forwarded email messages.
