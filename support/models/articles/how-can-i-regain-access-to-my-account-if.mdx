---
title: "How can I regain access to my account if I cannot receive a password reset email?"
keywords: ["User Management"]
---

If you can't receive a password reset email, try the following steps to regain access to your account:

1. **Check spam or junk folders:** Ensure the email isn't filtered there.
2. **Verify your email:** Confirm you're using the email associated with the account.
3. **Check for SSO options:** Use services such as "Sign in with Google" if available.
4. **Contact support:** If issues persist, contact support@wandb.com and provide your username and email.
