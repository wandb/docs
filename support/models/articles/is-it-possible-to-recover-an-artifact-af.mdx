---
title: "Is it possible to recover an artifact after it has been deleted with a run?"
keywords: ["Artifacts"]
---

No. When you delete a run, a prompt asks whether to delete the associated artifacts. If you choose this option, W&B permanently deletes the artifacts. You can't recover them, even if you later restore the run.
