Students can apply for an academic plan by following these steps:

- Visit the [Pricing page on wandb.com](https://wandb.ai/site/pricing) and apply for the academic plan.
- Start with a 30-day trial and convert it to an academic plan on the [W&B academic application page](https://wandb.ai/academic_application).
