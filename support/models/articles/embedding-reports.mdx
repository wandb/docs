This page describes how to share a W&B report by embedding it in another page, such as a blog post, internal wiki, or external website. Embedding lets readers view your report in context without leaving the host page.

At the top right of your report, click **Share**. At the bottom of the popup window, copy the embed code. Paste this code into the HTML of the page where you want the report to appear.
