---
title: "Does your tool track or store training data?"
keywords: ["Experiments"]
---

To associate a dataset with a training run, pass a SHA or unique identifier to `wandb.Run.config.update(...)`. W&B stores no data unless you call `wandb.Run.save()` with the local filename.
