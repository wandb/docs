---
title: "If wandb crashes, will it possibly crash my training run?"
keywords: ["Run Crashes"]
---

W&B runs in a separate process, so if W&B crashes, your training run continues. During an internet outage, W&B keeps retrying to send data to [wandb.ai](https://wandb.ai).
