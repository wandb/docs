---
title: "What is the difference between team and entity? As a user - what does entity mean for me?"
keywords: ["Team Management"]
---

A team is a collaborative workspace for users working on the same projects. An entity is either a username or a team name. When you log runs in W&B, set the entity to a personal or team account using `wandb.init(entity="example-team")`.
