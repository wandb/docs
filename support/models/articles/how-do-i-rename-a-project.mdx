---
title: "How do I rename a project?"
keywords: ["Projects"]
---

To rename a project:

1. Navigate to the **Project overview**.
2. Click **Edit Project**.

If the project name is protected, such as `model-registry`, it can't be renamed. Contact support for assistance with protected names.
