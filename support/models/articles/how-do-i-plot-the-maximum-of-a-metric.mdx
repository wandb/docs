---
title: "How do I plot the maximum of a metric?"
keywords: ["Metrics"]
---

To plot the running maximum of a metric:

1. Create a scatter plot of the metric.
2. Open the **Edit** menu and select **Annotations**.
3. Plot the running maximum of the values.
