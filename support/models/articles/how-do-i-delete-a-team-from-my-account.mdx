---
title: "How do I delete a team from my account?"
keywords: ["Administrator", "Team Management"]
---

To delete a team from an account:

1. Log in as a team admin.
2. Go to **Team settings** (accessible from the team page in the W&B UI).
3. Scroll to the bottom of the settings page and click **Delete team**.
4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

**⚠️ Warning: deleting your only team locks your account**

W&B accounts must belong to at least one team to create projects and run experiments. If you delete the only team associated with your account — including the personal team that W&B creates automatically when you sign up — you will lose the ability to create a new team through the UI. You may also see a "rate limit exceeded" error loop when you try to log in.

This state cannot be resolved without contacting W&B support to manually create a team for your account.

If you want to rename your team rather than delete it, note that team names cannot be changed after creation. Contact support before deleting so the transition can be handled without service interruption.

See [I deleted my team and now I can't create a new one — what do I do?](/support/models/articles/i-deleted-my-team-and-now-i-cant-create-a-new-one) for recovery steps.
