---
title: "How do I delete a team from my account?"
keywords: ["Administrator", "Team Management"]
---

As a team admin, you can permanently delete a team from your W&B account. Use this procedure when you need to remove a team that you no longer use.

To delete a team from your account:

1. Access team settings as an admin.
2. At the bottom of the page, click **Delete**.

W&B removes the team from your account.
