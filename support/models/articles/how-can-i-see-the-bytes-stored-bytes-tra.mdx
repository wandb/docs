---
title: "How can I see the bytes stored, bytes tracked and tracked hours of my organization?"
keywords: ["Administrator"]
---

Organization administrators can review bytes stored, bytes tracked, and tracked hours on the **Usage** page in organization settings. Use this information to monitor your organization's consumption in the current billing period.

To view your organization's usage:

1. Navigate to your organization's settings at `https://wandb.ai/account-settings/[ORGANIZATION-NAME]/settings`. Replace `[ORGANIZATION-NAME]` with your organization's name.
2. Select the **Billing** tab.
3. In the **Usage this billing period** section, select the **View usage** button.

The **Usage** page displays your organization's bytes stored, bytes tracked, and tracked hours for the current billing period.

<Note>
Tracked hours are unlimited for all plans and are not billed. Tracked hours are displayed on the **Usage** page for monitoring purposes only.
</Note>
