---
title: "Incorporating LaTeX"
keywords: ["Reports"]
---

You can include LaTeX equations in reports to document mathematical notation alongside your notes, custom visualizations, and tables. To add LaTeX, create a new report and begin typing in the rich text area.

Then, to insert LaTeX content, start a new line, press `/`, and navigate to the **Inline equations** tab.
