---
title: "What if I want to integrate W&B into my project, but I don't want to upload any images or media?"
keywords: ["Experiments"]
---

W&B supports projects that log only scalars by letting you specify which files or data to upload. For an example of logging without images, see this [PyTorch example](https://wandb.me/pytorch-colab).
