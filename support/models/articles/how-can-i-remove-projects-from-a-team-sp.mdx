---
title: "How can I remove projects from a team space without admin privileges?"
keywords: ["Administrator"]
---

To remove projects from a team space without admin privileges, try one of the following approaches:

- Request that a current admin remove the projects.
- Ask the admin to grant temporary access for project management.

If you can't reach the admin, contact a billing admin or another authorized user in your organization.
