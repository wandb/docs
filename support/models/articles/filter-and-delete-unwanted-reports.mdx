---
title: "Filter and delete unwanted reports"
keywords: ["Reports"]
---

To keep a project organized, remove reports you no longer need. Use the search bar to filter the reports list, then do one of the following:

- To delete a single report, select the unwanted report.
- To delete multiple reports, select all reports and click **Delete Reports**.
