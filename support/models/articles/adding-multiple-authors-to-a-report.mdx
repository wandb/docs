---
title: "Adding multiple authors to a report"
keywords: ["Reports"]
---

Credit all contributors in your report by adding multiple authors. This approach is useful when a report represents the work of a team rather than one person.

To add multiple authors, click the **+** icon next to the name of the author. The **+** icon opens a drop-down menu listing all users who have access to the report. Select the users you want to add as authors. The selected users now appear as co-authors on the report.
