---
title: "How can I delete my user account?"
keywords: ["User Management"]
---

To delete your user account, go to your [user settings](/platform/app/settings-page/user-settings#delete-your-account) and click **Delete account**.

<Warning>
This action is irreversible and takes effect immediately.
</Warning>
