---
title: wandb job-describe
---


Describe a launch job. Provide the launch job in the form of: entity/project/job-name:alias-or-version

## Usage

```shell
wandb job describe [OPTIONS] JOB
```


## Options

### `help`

Show this message and exit.                

| Flag | Default | Type | 
|------|---------|------|
| `--help` | False | BOOL |

## CLI Help
