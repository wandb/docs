---
title: How can I compare images or media across epochs or steps?
---

Use **Compare mode** in a media panel to view 2 to 4 images or videos from different runs, steps, or indices in a single grid. Open a media panel with images or video, switch to **Compare mode**, then select the runs, steps, or indices to compare. Your selection is saved so you can return to the same comparison later. For details, see [Compare mode](/models/app/features/panels/media#compare-mode).

You can also expand the image panel and use the step slider to move through images from different steps in a single run.
