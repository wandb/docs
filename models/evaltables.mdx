title: Evaluation Tables
description: "Learn how to create, compare, and visualize evaluation tables in W&B."

Use Eval Tables to compare model versions and training steps side-by-side, see aggregate scores, investigate individual examples, find top-k regressions and more.

<Frame>
    <img src="/images/evaltables/mnist.png" alt="Evaluation table view" width="100%" />
</Frame>

W&B recommends converting your existing W&B Tables to Eval Tables for faster rendering and more features. See [Migrate from the old evaluation tables](/models/evaltables/create-an-evaluation-table#convert-a-w&b-table-to-an-eval-table) for instructions on how to convert your W&B Tables to Eval Tables.
